About

Kelly Chang is a creative programmer and game designer/developer. She graduated from Masters in Integrated Digital Media program at New York Univeristy in 2018. She is a hardcore programmer who uses Linux in her daily life, also plays electric guitar in a post-metal band. Her interests are in how games influence human behavior and how programming solves real life problems. In Fimbulwinter, she explores how board game can create social changes in political engagement.
